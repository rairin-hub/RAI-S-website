✨LORPAK✨

✨HANAZAWA✨

Soft lights, calm vibes, and the comforting aroma of freshly baked pastries fill the air.Step into a cozy Japanese-style café where every bite feels like a warm hug and every sip brings a moment of peace.

✨BAESHAKES✨

Baeshake is a cozy café serving authentic Korean dishes in generous portions at affordable prices. With calm vibes, a modern style, and soft Korean songs playing, it’s the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a taste of Korean food.

✨NGOPI✨

Ngopi is a warm, old-style café that brings nostalgic vibes to every visit. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee and delicious pastries fills the air, inviting everyone to sit back and enjoy. From pastries to sandwiches, it’s a favorite spot for all generations to gather, share stories, and enjoy good food together.

Visit NGOPI →
